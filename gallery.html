Tips and Tricks


This is one of my first projects. From this experience, I learned the importance of using a good flux.

After switching from liquid to paste flux, I was able to get smoother solder lines.



While soldering this chicken design, I tried using masking tape to hold the pieces together.

This was especially helpful when working with the smaller pieces shown here.



This is my most recent and most challenging piece.

While working on this project, I learned the importance of grinding each piece precisely so it fits together as envisioned, a step often overlooked.

As you can see if you look closely, some pieces weren't cut perfecrly, leaving me with an unsymmetrical final outcome.
