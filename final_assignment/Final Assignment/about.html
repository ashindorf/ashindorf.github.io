About Stained Glass

Stained glass is a type of art that has been around for centuries. It involves cutting and shaping pieces of colored glass to create designs and images. Stained glass is often used in windows, but it can also be used in other decorative items such as lamps and jewelry. The process of making stained glass involves several steps, including designing the pattern, cutting the glass, and assembling the pieces using lead or copper foil. Stained glass can be found in many different styles, from traditional to modern, and it continues to be a popular form of artistic expression today.
